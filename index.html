About us

Tutoring Hub is a South African-based academic support platform dedicated to empowering learners from high school to university. Supported by a team of educators, developers, and curriculum specialists, our mission is to make STEM subjects accessible, engaging, and achievable for all students.

We believe that every learner deserves personalized guidance, and every tutor deserves a platform to share their expertise. Tutoring Hub bridges this gap by offering live video sessions, AI-powered homework help, and tailored academic tracking—all in one place. Whether you're preparing for NSC exams or tackling varsity modules, our platform is built to support your journey.
